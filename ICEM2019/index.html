Why should you join?

ICEM is an annual event for both IAESTE trainees and members from all over the world. It is a must in your calendar. Come to Cracow and experience the climate of the magical Old Town and Vistula River. The city is the only one of its kind, offering a rich historic and cultural heritage, astounding sites and a wonderful atmosphere. Our Local Committee gives you the chance to spend an unforgettable weekend with various attractions, from a treasure hunting city game, to all night long parties. Meet awesome people and make unforgettable memories!

Information on making the payment:
  • Please note that you have to choose your plan in the registration form.
  • You will receive the payment details via e-mail.
  • We only accept bank transfers.
  • After your registration, you have 3 days to make your payment. Otherwise you will be removed from the participant's list.
  • Please make sure to cover all transfer costs.
Every participant can freely and without any restrictions resign from participating in the ICEM Cracow Weekend 2019 until the 26th of July. All costs will be refunded. If you resign until the 1st of August, only 50% of your costs will be refunded. Resigning after the 1st of August makes a refund impossible.
